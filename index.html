About me

In the journey of coding, discovery, and relentless curiosity, I’m a computer science student deeply rooted in the art and science of technology. I spend my days not just learning but embracing the relentless pursuit of knowledge, whether it’s through group projects, thesis research, or late-night coding marathons. My vision is to become a software engineer—one who not only understands the intricacies of code but also the essence of innovation. Behind the projects and assignments, I’m a passionate builder. I find joy in turning concepts into reality, like the cake shop web application I crafted from scratch — PASTELCO —where I spent countless sleepless nights refining each detail, pouring everything I had into every line of code. My drive extends beyond academia; I’m an executive member (Chairperson) of IEEE CS BRAC University SBC - and the Publication Coordinator of the IEEE Computer Society Bangladesh Team SPARK, striving to inspire others in the tech community. From organizing events like 'Full-Stack Development with MERN' to setting up community platforms for academic support, my aim is to create a space where knowledge flows freely and curiosity never stops. I also love the thrill of the quantum realm, having secured a spot in QClass24/25, eager to expand my horizons into the field of Quantum Machine Learning. This is me, standing at the crossroad of code, creativity, and endless possibilities, crafting a future where technology and imagination intertwine.
